About Us

Umpleby Motorcycles is an authorised Suzuki Motorcycles Dealership. Originally established as Suzuki Durban, we have undergone a change in ownership and refreshing rebrand. Improvements and adjustments are necessary in always ensuring superior customer experiences.

We specialise in high-quality care and maintenance for motorcycles. Dedicated to excellence, we provide unparalleled service to our customers with a focus on quality, Genuine Suzuki Parts, technical expertise, and top-tier customer support.

About the Owner

Umpleby Motorocycles is owned by Jarred Umpleby. More than our Owner, Jarred is also a proud husband and father of two beautiful children - Isla and Roarke. A biker himself, Jarred is retired from an impressive professional Motocross career spanning a total of 23 years, which explains his deep passion for the motorcycle industry. With Jarred, you are in trusted and experienced hands.
